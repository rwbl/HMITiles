#### 🚨 Lighthouse Simulator (`LighthouseSimulator.b4x`)
A unique automation example modeling a coastal marine lighthouse station console.
* Diurnal Light Cycle: Simulates an automated daylight photocell loop where ambient light (Lux) naturally transitions up and down, putting the system into DAYTIME IDLE or enabling night operations.
* Optical Timing Signatures: Implements precise modulo clock cycles to replicate an authentic maritime "Isophase" optical light flash signature (equal 2-second periods of light and dark cycles).
* Environmental Triggers & Interlocks: Features an automated pneumatic foghorn system that activates intermittent sound blasts during low-visibility weather spikes (> 65% Fog Density) unless blocked by a tactical maintenance override.
* Mechanical Stress Analysis: Demonstrates process safety interlocking where forcing the main drive motor to rotate the heavy Fresnel lens assembly during daylight lockout constraints triggers high-visibility mechanical bearing temperature alarms.
